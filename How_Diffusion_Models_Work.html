How Diffusion Models Work - Syllabus

Deepen your knowledge in diffusion-based generative AI and build your own model from scratch.

Course Syllabus

  • Introduction

    Duration: 2 mins

    Overview of diffusion models and their applications in generative AI.

  • Intuition

    Duration: 4 mins

    Understanding the basics and intuition behind the diffusion process.

  • Sampling

    Duration: 7 mins (includes code examples)

    Hands-on exploration of sampling techniques in diffusion models.

  • Neural Network

    Duration: 3 mins (includes code examples)

    Building and implementing neural networks for noise prediction in diffusion models.

  • Training

    Duration: 6 mins (includes code examples)

    Training diffusion models with practical lab exercises.

  • Controlling

    Duration: 5 mins (includes code examples)

    Adding control to diffusion models for tailored outputs and personalized generation.

  • Speeding Up

    Duration: 4 mins (includes code examples)

    Optimizing diffusion model sampling to enhance performance by up to 10x.

  • Summary

    Duration: 1 min

    Key takeaways and a recap of the diffusion modeling process.
