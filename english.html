English Grammar Course Syllabus

A1 Level - Beginner

Focus: Basic sentence construction, familiar vocabulary, and essential grammar structures.

  • Parts of Speech: Nouns (basic types: common, proper), Pronouns (basic personal pronouns), Articles (a, an, the), Basic Adjectives (colors, sizes, quantities)
  • Verb Basics: Introduction to Verbs (am, is, are, has, have), Present Simple, Imperatives
  • Sentence Basics: Basic Sentence Structure, Simple Questions, Simple Negatives
  • Prepositions: Basic Prepositions (in, on, at, near, under)
  • Conjunctions: Basic Conjunctions (and, but, or)

A2 Level - Elementary

Focus: Expanding sentence structures, basic tenses, more prepositions and conjunctions.

  • Advanced Nouns and Pronouns: Countable and Uncountable Nouns, Possessive Pronouns
  • Verbs and Tenses: Present Continuous, Past Simple (regular verbs)
  • Adjectives and Adverbs: Adjective Order, Basic Adverbs of Time and Place
  • Prepositions and Conjunctions: Time Prepositions (in, on, at), Conjunctions (because, so)
  • Simple Sentences: Making Simple and Compound Sentences, Question Words

B1 Level - Intermediate

Focus: Complex sentence formation, various verb tenses, and introductory conditional sentences.

  • Complex Nouns and Pronouns: Reflexive Pronouns, Relative Pronouns
  • Advanced Verbs and Tenses: Past Continuous, Future Simple, Modals (can, could, should)
  • Conditionals: Zero and First Conditionals
  • Adjectives and Adverbs: Comparative and Superlative Forms, Adverbs of Frequency
  • Sentence Structure: Direct and Indirect Speech (introduction), Phrasal Verbs (basic usage)

B2 Level - Upper Intermediate

Focus: Advanced grammar concepts, complex structures, and perfect tenses.

  • Advanced Pronouns and Determiners: Indefinite Pronouns, Quantifiers
  • Verb Tenses and Moods: Present Perfect, Past Perfect, Future Continuous
  • Complex Conditionals: Second Conditional
  • Adjectives and Adverbs: Intensifiers, Advanced Adverb Placement
  • Direct and Indirect Speech: Reporting Statements and Questions
  • Phrasal Verbs and Idioms: Advanced Phrasal Verbs, Common Idioms

C1 Level - Advanced

Focus: Mastery of complex grammar, nuanced tenses, and sophisticated language structures.

  • Complex Sentence Structures: Relative Clauses, Passive Voice (all tenses)
  • Verb Tenses and Moods: Present Perfect Continuous, Past Perfect Continuous, Third Conditional
  • Advanced Modals and Hypotheticals: Modal Perfects, Hypotheticals and Wishes
  • Advanced Adjectives and Adverbs: Collocations, Emphatic Intensifiers
  • Direct and Indirect Speech: Advanced Reporting Verbs
  • Idioms and Expressions: Common English Idioms, Contextual Idiomatic Expressions
